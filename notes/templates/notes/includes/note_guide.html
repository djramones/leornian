  • You can format your note using a limited set of Markdown syntaxes. Specifically, the following are enabled: emphasis (italic & bold, using asterisks or underscores), strikethrough (with tildes), inline code (with backticks), HTML entities, backslash escapes, and ordered & unordered lists.
  • Use the preview function to check if your note is formatted as intended, before submitting.
  • You cannot edit a note once it has been submitted.
  • You can delete a submitted note unless another user has saved it to their collection; however, you can remove attribution from your account at any time (i.e., the note will become anonymous and disassociated from your account).
  • Setting your note’s visibility setting to “Unlisted” makes it less visible to other users, for example by not appearing in Discover nor in your author page, but it does not make your note private (anyone with the note’s link will still be able to access it).
  • You can change your note’s visibility at any time after submission (unless a content moderator locks the note’s visibility setting).
  • Please be mindful of the license terms that apply to your submitted content.
